About Todo App

Todo App is a simple and intuitive application designed to help you organize your tasks and manage your daily activities. Whether you need to create a to-do list for work, school, or personal tasks, Todo App has got you covered.

With Todo App, you can easily add, edit, and delete tasks, mark them as completed, and stay organized throughout the day. The user-friendly interface allows for quick and efficient task management, ensuring you never miss an important deadline again.
